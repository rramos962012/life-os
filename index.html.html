🌿 How to use Life OS
1
Capture everything

Use the ☰ menu (top right) to instantly capture any thought, task, or idea. Assign it a domain and priority. It lands in your Inbox.

2
Work the Dashboard

The Dashboard tab shows your 10 domains, balance indicator, and weekly focus. Click any domain card to open its full Domain Focus view.

3
Each domain has 4 lists

Inside Domain Focus: Active Projects (things with a next action), Waiting On (delegated/pending), Someday/Maybe (ideas not yet committed), and Reference (manuals, warranties, records, contacts).

4
Productivity = Inbox + Tasks + Projects + Register + Assets

The Productivity tab consolidates all your actionable items. Use sub-tabs to switch between views. Tasks are filtered by the active profile (Rob / Crystal / Family).

5
Profile switcher

Rob sees his tasks + shared household tasks. Crystal sees hers + shared. Family view shows everything. Switch profiles from the Dashboard or Tasks tab.

6
Review rhythms

Daily (15 min): process inbox + update next actions. Weekly (60 min): full domain sweep + push to Notion. Quarterly (2–3 hrs): project audit + register check + 4 Core Lists sweep.
